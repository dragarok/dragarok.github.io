Smiling like a madman

First I was innocent,
I asked for an identity,
The world pushed me down and pained me.
I talked to the world about this pain,
And the world mocked me.


I felt alone in this fight,
I wanted to run away.
But, she didn't let me.
She made me break into a thousand pieces,
But always took great care that none was lost.
And when I gave up,
She made me work on myself.
And That's how I recollected myself to be wholler than the whole.


-------------------------------xx-------------------------------


Second I was in limelight,
I asked for strength,
Then the eyes of the world fixated at me pained me.
Their dreams, their expectations,
Them trying to fulfill their dreams through me.
I again talked to the world about the burden of expectations,
And the world really didn't care.

I felt alone again in this fight,
I wanted to run away.
But, she didn't let me.
She made me take even more burden until I broke again,
Yet she took great care again that none was lost.
And when I gave up,
She made me work on myself.
And That's how I recollected myself to be wholler than the whole again.


-------------------------------xx-------------------------------


Third, I was now strong enough to take any burden.
I asked for truth,
I started wandering here and there in search of truth,
The words from thousand masters buzzed like a bomb inside my head.
But by now, I knew the world never listened.
So I chose a few I was hoping would listen without judgement,
Tried telling them about the never ending search,
Yet they too didn’t get much.



I was feeling utterly alone by then,
I wanted to run away.
But, she didn't let me.
I had to sit there with thousand petals opening inside me,
Yet not even an eye to appreciate.
I wanted to help those few to make it bloom in them,
Yet each one had their own doubts.


The petals bloomed,
The petals faded,
And they fell back to the ground.
Forgetting the fact that it is the nature of petals to fall off,
I started crying seeing all those petals within withering away,
Yet she took great care to collect them all for me.
And when I gave up crying,
She held my hand,
She reached out to me with the incense she prepared from those flowers,
And That’s how I recollected myself to be wholler than the whole again.



-------------------------------xx-------------------------------


Fourth, I realized myself and my relationship with her.
Through her eyes,
I realized the world too was her all along.
Now, I was no longer bashing my head,
Neither was I trying to make the world understand me.
I was happy with the fragrance she left me with.


One day, I realized she had been with me the whole time,
And then, I no longer needed to run away.
I no longer needed to say anything to the world,
Or hear back something from there.
I no longer was bashing my head.
I was just there lost in the fragrance of the flowers,
And she came.
She asked me to not get lost in that fragrance,
And gave me keys to her garden itself.


Now, I sit there smiling like a madman. ☺️
