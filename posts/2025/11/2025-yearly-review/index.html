2025 Yearly Review

“!!” I want to start with an exclamation. This year has been one of that year that I had been waiting for decades to happen to me. From the year 2023, it finally crossed the Tipping Point and started getting crazier. There were parts about my identity that I really got to explore, go deep into, and really uncover myself piece by piece. Like going petal by petal in a lotus flower. ü™∑

In the year 2024, I got the first clear glimpse. In the year 2025, I cleared my doubts, and learned about my path. In the year 2026, the path will be done, and I feel that a new journey is already waiting for me. I don’t know how/what it will be, but it is surely going to demand more energy and vigor from my part.

In my own words, I wrote in my yearly review for 2024: “2023 was speeding with insights. Its been a slow year compared to 2023 because everything was about calming down.” That’s why 2025 happened, going supersonic. The questions I said that will get answered in 2024’s review got answered as well.

I have started using emacs more with almost exact setup on my phone as well. Really tops Obsidian, Notion or any of that stuff because its the same workflow that I have on my mac as well. Its so much elegant now.

These are my notes in org roam. It looks really beautiful and getting more and more connected each year. I wait for the day when I can throw it all on a AR setup and traverse through nodes like planets and galaxies in games.

Finally, I found who I was in relation to this world. After some time, no place was distant. I was at home almost all the time wherever I was. It got to the point when I knew that I wasn’t the only witness to my life.

It all started with a firm conviction. A plan that I had for a long long time; one that my being had been waiting for, unaware without an idea of what would be coming. Its as if I projected one of my very first and favorite novel onto my life: “The Alchemist.” I even listened to that book in Nepali saying “Kimiyagar”. Just like him, I didn’t know why but an unknown force pulled me towards an unknown place. I felt stupid at times to be walking in the dark, but in the end, that journey was meant to be mine; just like Santiago’s journey was meant to be his and his only. Yes, he would have been deemed a fool if the story didn’t end in riches, but who is keeping the tabs anyway?

I left my home in search of answers that I’d been seeking. I knew I had to walk this path alone, and it became so. This was the very first moment of my life where I actually left “home”. The first 10 minutes of it, I can almost taste all of it even today. That journey was a lot. I started with my expectations of where I would go, but let myself be guided freely. I ended up in places I hadn’t even planned to go. A lot of unknowns, yet with a sense of familiarity in conviction. Every place started like a stranger that I wanted to run away from asap, but ended up knowing that they’ve been my besties all along. They made me cry as well as cried on my shoulder like family.

I was looking for specific something. In that process I came to know, the best of things are not meant to be looked for. The best of things find you. You don’t find them. This is a very contradictory thing to say, yet its one of the most truthful things I can tell you. Think of it like this: Clear your pond of all the dirt that you have accumulated over the years. Nature will come to adorn your pond: birds, fishes etc. But if you just justify your laziness and your pond gets dirty, inaction will kill all the vibes. So, non-action is the key, not inaction. And there is a night and day of a difference between these two.

I saw both good and worse parts of humanity in same place. I saw truthful and liars in same robes. I saw power and surrender in same place. I felt fear and courage in same place. I saw death and beauty in same place. I heard the hardest of cries and playful laughter in the same place. I was silent and beaming with answers in same place. The duality of it, almost as if everything was a stage, set for my eyes to understand reality in its full glory. And it was just the start of 2025.

I travelled far and wide. I cried my eyes out in pain. I laughed with all my heart. I cried my eyes out in peace. I saw it all, that too in a very short time span. Literally made me binge it all in one go.

This was what I wrote in my diary when I returned: “When I truly seek, I have always been found. Thanks to everyone for loving me like its favorite child and treating me this way. I will do everything I can to give as much as I can. I know my actions for my personal seeking has been done. Those will be minor part of a major life force. I will be the torch bearer of life itself. The essence. The fragrance. I will make sure that I smell like life itself every day.”

The convictions that found me led me to finding so much more. Overflowing with insights, I couldn’t even keep track of things. Answers coming at supersonic speed. There were years where I struggled to even get one. This was year where I got answers to those questions that I didn’t know I had deep longing for.

I’d chosen Purple üü£ for 2025, and that was not a coincidence. That was a conviction that could have only come from within. And life truly showed me that color. Now, I know what I needed to know. There are things that I know that I don’t know, and its okay. There are things that I don’t know that I don’t know, and that’s okay too.

So much convoluted talk. Basically, the summary is I finally got answers that I’ve been searching for all my life. Exactly like a lost child finds home. For 2026, there is no color, no shape, no form, no name. Just presence speaking for itself. One intention speaking: “Be.” “Be.” Not A, not C. Just “Be.” ü´†

And I realized one major problem with my concepts that I had last year. The drawing that I had on my last year review about what creation/enlightenment failed to take into account the infinite nature of being. “The infinity”. There is no circle that binds either creation or enlightened beings. There is no Yin and Yang because its empty and boundless at the same time. Just like you cannot count the moments you lived even if you tried to break it down. You will end up going in an infinite regress on what counts as a moment. Just like Cantor showed us uncountable infinities with whole numbers.
