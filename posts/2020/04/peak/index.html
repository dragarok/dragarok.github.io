One minute

Peak

Peak they saw,

Thought they made,

Of the day when their steps would be on the mountain peak,

Clear seem’d they, Of the goal they desperately seek.

 

Guided by the river trail,

Dreams flew high against the flow,

The mountains of hope: deep and strong first,

Now saw the wind of fear that slowly blow.

 

The path feels rough now,

The silent howls of people’s voice he heard,

Of those who got lost on that way,

And of those who sought to walk away.

 

His heart gets cold,

The body gets blue,

Creeping from the inside out,

Growls the wolf wishing to devour his vow.
