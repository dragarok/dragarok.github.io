Controlling Irrational Thoughts

Solutions

ID: 14c476ff-c39c-47f8-a6f4-cd08e38f4ea1
  1. Mindfulness
    • Knowing what you are thinking of and what is happening internally will help you understand how irrational thoughts surface and develop with time.
    • To help with thought interruptions, keep a timer, rubber band or something that constantly stays with you and add it to your lifestyle purposefully for that mental activity interruption.
  2. Gratitude
    • Feel good for all the small yet beautiful things in life. This helps build positive hormones in the brain.
  3. Challenge irrationalities
    • When you find yourself nagging without any evidence, challenge yourself to find evidences for it. Bring logic and rationality to the picture; will help you realize the futility of a thought drain.
  4. Meditate
    • Any way of meditation is helpful for your mind since it’s the time you accept to recognize yourself without subconsciously living a life and accepting everything that’s out there. It will help you get insights, be more peaceful as well.
  5. Visualization
    • Visualization are amazing tools of positive affirmations. If you can imagine yourself doing and believe it, it will help you attract that effect. Imagine yourself being confident just like a mantra to repeat at certain times. This will push yourself to work as well.
