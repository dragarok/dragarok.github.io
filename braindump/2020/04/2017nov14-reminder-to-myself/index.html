A memo to myself

First everyday see yourself that you are not getting yourself back into the old habit patterns of the mind and make sure you are quietly working well to get something out as well.

  1. Talk about the three month plan.
  2. Plan for the three month work in a clear way and find as mininmum distractions as possible.
  3. Try to find yourself more comfortable in sharing rather than contemplating inside ownself.
  4. Let her flow rather than to force her to get herself to a good position.
  5. Be sarcastic never hurtful.
  6. Everyday find time to meditate in the evenings and in the mornings.
  7. Never try to find yourself imagining doing everything at the same time.
  8. Try to find things in a better way. Promote people to take something out of them rather than just feel like everyone is incompetent and not doing their work properly.
  9. Be beautiful by heart.
  10. Have an amazing personality, dressing and feel like yes it’s the way I want to be.
  11. Remember to at least watch it when you contemplate boy.
