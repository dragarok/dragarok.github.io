Broadcasting Arrays

  • Broadcast happens when the sizes of matrix operation doesn’t meet. e.g. adding 3 by 3 matrix with 1 by 3 makes the 1 by 3 repeat 3 times and add with all rows.

  • Shapes need to be compatible in order for broadcast to occur.

  • When operating on two arrays, NumPy compares their shapes element-wise. It starts with the trailing dimensions, and works its way forward.

  • Two dimensions are compatible when they are equal, or one of them is 1
